Commission on Reforms in the Education System of India

With many changes taking place over the past few years, the Indian Education System has suffered a lot of turmoil. This Ad-Hoc Commission aims to deliberate on these changes. Starting from the complaints about the removal of CCE, to the proper enactment of the RTE Act, this committee aims to discuss all plausible scenarios and their solutions. This commission expects delegates to step into the shoes of some of the most respectable and honorable personalities. The need of the hour is to reform the education system and delegates must propose rational and implementable solutions to the problems addressed in committee. The Executive Board, consisting of 4 members, is geared up and looks forward to active participation. The agenda has been selected keeping in mind the pressing issues of the present education system of India. The intensity of debate and diverse representation is surely a compelling reason to participate in this commission. The experienced, enthusiastic Executive Board welcomes you to the much awaited:

CRESI - 2018!
