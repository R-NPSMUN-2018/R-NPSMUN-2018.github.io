About R-NPSMUN

NPS Rajajinagar proudly presents to you the first edition of R-NPSMUN 2018!!!

With an amalgamation of the consistent committees fused with a few unconventional ones, we have ensured that each delegate, new or experienced, has something to look forward to. Delegates will have to think on their feet to keep up with the pace of the debate, combating the obstacles posed, while sticking to the portfolios represented. Alliances will be made, strategies devised, while keeping the nations’ best interests at heart. Factual knowledge along with rationale arbitrations will sail you through!

So delegates, be a part of this exhilarating experience *and something* !

Deepika Karanji

“Describe yourself” we say. “Well, I’m just a geek who happens to game, pun, and mun!” she laughs.

Easily approachable and very affable, with her as your secretary general, we hope all you delegates have a wonderful time at R-NPSMUN’18!

Letter from the Secretery General

Dear Delegates,
It is my pleasure to welcome you all to the very first edition of R-NPSMUN! I am Deepika Karanji, and I will be serving as your Secretary General.


In our very first year, we are proud to feature a variety of committees, including the DISEC, Security Council, the Axis Tripartite, the CRESI, the International Press Corps, and the very happening FIFA Council.
The committees have something exciting to offer to every delegate, because not only is it going to be a great learning experience, but the committees are also going to host high quality, intense debate. Each committee is going to be handled by an amazing and a highly qualified panel. Every agenda has been set to give the sessions a thought-provoking and enthralling path toward what we expect to be very complex and realistic resolutions. This way we create leaders for our future country that are sure to be an analytical and studious generation with lots to give to the world.
As delegates, all you have to do, is research well, prepare to face top-notch debates, and most importantly, Have Fun! Leave the rest to us, and we will do our best to ensure that each and every one of you has a wonderful time here.

Registrations are now open, and more details regarding dates, venue, and committees will be available on our website , which will be continuously updated in the months leading up to the conference. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

As the head of the Executive Board, I once again welcome you all to R-NPSMUN 2018! Let the debates begin!

Deepika Karanji
Secretary General

Letter from the Under Secretery General

“The secret of change is to focus all of your energy, not on fighting the old, but on building the new.”

~Socrates~

Socrates’ philosophical importance lies not only in the fact that he gave us the theory of Conflict and its perpetual existence in our lives; but he also made us in turn realize, that there will always be a need for resolution.

It gives me immense pleasure to welcome all of you to this first edition of R-NPSMUN. We, the Executive Board of R-NPSMUN 2018, invite you to come be part of an experience that promises to be engaging, eventful and educational. The Executive Board, have put together an exciting array of Committees that attempt to tackle issues of a very high pace. One of the big questions of a Model United Nations, is why? Why do we simulate six committees that take on the political and economic issues that plague the international community at present, and in the past, among a community of students? This is because at National Public School RNR, we strive to achieve excellence. We wish to provide a platform for great, young minds to be ambitious and work towards being leaders of a better tomorrow.

As the Under Secretary General, I personally invite Delegates to broaden their minds and really step into the shoes of Officers of the countries they represent. This is a fantastic opportunity to gain a first-hand working knowledge of how the UN works and how the structural framework of this great body, can be used to maintain world peace. Let this not be an opportunity to just gain knowledge, but an opportunity to apply that knowledge from a legal and practical perspective. If you, as a delegate can do something to effect real change in the world, I invite you to explore, how this can be achieved, and on what basis, under what framework and in what capacity, and give me all these aspects together. That, is the mark of an excellent Delegate, and we will accept no less, here at R-NPSMUN 2018.

For it is not just the motto of NPSRNR, but also a slogan that we can stand behind, as we simulate the World community./

Reach Out, Reach High, Reach Beyond!!!

Chinmaya P
Under Secretary General
