THE FIFA COUNCIL

“FIFA cannot sit by and see greed rule the football world. Nor shall we.” -Sepp Blatter The heart of the entire footballing world, The FIFA Council is equipped to deal with everything related to the extravagant tournament . It is this body that upholds what football stands for - Teamwork and Unity. Here at R- NPSMUN, the FIFA Council will deal with the controversies of the Qatar World Cup 2022. In 2010, FIFA astounded the world when it bestowed upon the small Persian Gulf State of Qatar the opportunity to host the 2022 World Cup. Qatar’s winning bid to host FIFA 2022 World Cup has been controversial due to allegations of bribery and reports by Amnesty International detailing the mistreatment of migrant workers building the stadiums for the World Cup.
