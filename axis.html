AXIS TRIPARTITE

The year is 1943. Dark times. The world is right in the middle of the greatest war it has seen, and there seems to be no end. In this committee, you shall fight the war, and fight to win. The catch: you’re on the losing side of history. You are on the side of history whose actions haunt us to this day. The Axis Cabinet starts on 1st July 1943, just a few days before Operation Husky was initiated to invade Sicily, Italy. Suffice to say, all is not well. A body seemingly belonging to that of a British Royal Marine Pilot has been recovered off the Spanish coast; it contained documents enough to convince Hitler that the invasion was in the direction of Greece or Sardinia-Piedmont. But within the Italian military, some believe that the documents were hoax and the invasion is to take place elsewhere…within Italy. And they’d like to be prepared for it.

Welcome to the Axis Bunker!

Each delegate will represent a military leader, a political leader, a dictator, a king or maybe even an Empress. The Axis Tripartite is focusing on the Allied Invasion of Sicily. Come change the course of history. Gauge your alliances. Form them and break them. Prepare to encounter obstacles at every turn.

But,  do not forget :

Victory to the Axis !
